Talk plan:
    title: [AI will take your lunch.]
    subtitle: [Learn to work with AI before it learns to work around you.]

The key points are:
    1. Shock intro: (Propose ideas here? AI replacing something?)
    1. AI is here, and will be completely changing the job landscape.
    2. They must learn to use AI on their own initiative
        a. Even the universities are not sure how to use AI
        b. For most industries, its not even clear how AI will be used in 3 years' time.
    3. They should learn to work with AI
    4. Start experimenting now!
        a. Get a ChatGPT/Claude/similar account.
        b. Make it write code, make art, music
        c. Give it some of your problems to work on.
        d. See if you can break somehow -- see what you can do to fix it?
            a. Get AI
        e. See if you can automate something with it.
            a. Build a skill or something.

Audience: 17 and 18 year olds in Singapore, JC/K-11 and -12 students, before they sit for their final exams and head to university.

Duration: 15 minutes
